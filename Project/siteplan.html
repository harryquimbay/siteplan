UmiShima Site Plan

Site Name

UmiShima - A modern beachwear store offering stylish and comfortable clothing for ocean lovers. The name combines Japanese words for "ocean" (Umi) and "island" (Shima), reflecting relaxation, nature, and luxury.

Optional domain: umishimabeachwear.com

Site Purpose

The site will showcase beach clothing collections, highlight seasonal trends, and provide an online shop for customers. It will also feature brand values, style inspiration, and contact options. The goal is to offer a clean, luxurious browsing experience that makes online shopping seamless.

Scenarios

  • What beach clothing collections are available this season?
  • Where can I find eco-friendly fabric options?
  • How can I contact UmiShima for customer support or sizing questions?

Color Schema

These colors were chosen to create a luxurious, ocean-inspired design:

#005B73
#FF7F50
#CBB29A
#F3F0E1

Usage: - #005B73: Headings, main text - #FF7F50: Accents, highlights, buttons - #CBB29A: Subtle accents and dividers - #F3F0E1: Background

Typography

  • Merriweather: Headings (luxurious serif style)
  • Roboto: Body text (clean, modern sans-serif)
  • Playfair Display: Logo and brand highlights
